SyzygyySTORAGE


We use blockchain technology to provide highest levels of data security with industy standard End-to-End encryption. Instead of running our own servers like the conventional cloud service providers, we allow other users to contribute their own storage and we pay them according to how much of their shared storage is being used. This means we can provide storage at a lower price compared to traditional cloud storage service providers, and there is also no worries of your data being read by the cloud providers. If you are worrying about the users reading your data, no worries, your data is split into multiple parts, then encrypted before it is stored on the servers. Since only you will have the keys, no one else will have access to your data.

Features

End-to-End Encryption


We have end-to-end encryption for all of your files. When end-to-end encrypted, all your files are secured from falling into the wrong hands.

Higher Availability


We store your files by splitting it into parts on multiple servers across the globe. This eliminates any single point of failure and ensures highest possible uptime, on par with any other cloud storage solutions.

Blazing Fast


Since the files are stored on multiple servers, it helps to avoid slow download or upload speed from one place. Making it fast and reliable.

Open-Source Software


Our project is completely open source. Many individuals have contributed to our project, and there is an active community building innovative applications on top of the our API.
