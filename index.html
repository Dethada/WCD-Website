SyzygyySTORAGE

Improved Privacy

SyzygyySTORAGE encrypts your files and distributes them across a decentralized network. Since only you hold the keys, you own your data. No outside company can access or control your files including us. This Provides a much safer environment for your data as compared to traditional cloud storage providers.

Affordable Compared to Traditional Solutions

SyzygyySTORAGE's decentralized cloud is on average 20x less expensive than current cloud storage providers. Storing 1TB on SyzygyySTORAGE costs about 10% less, compared with other storage solutions.
